What is Brick Breaker ?

Brick Breaker is a classic arcade game that brings back nostalgia while offering a modern twist. The objective of the game is simple yet addictive: break all the bricks using a paddle and a ball. Each brick you break earns you points, with each brick worth 100 points.

History of the Game

Brick Breaker traces its roots back to the arcade era of the late 1970s and early 1980s. It gained immense popularity with the release of various versions on early home gaming consoles. Over the years, it has evolved and adapted to different platforms, retaining its appeal to players of all ages.

How to Play

The gameplay is straightforward. You control a paddle that moves horizontally at the bottom of the screen. Using the paddle, you deflect a bouncing ball towards rows of bricks arranged at the top of the screen. Your goal is to break all the bricks by bouncing the ball off the paddle without letting it fall below. If the ball falls below the paddle, you lose a life. However, you can earn extra lives and power-ups by breaking certain bricks or achieving specific milestones.
